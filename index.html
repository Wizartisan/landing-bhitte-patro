Troubleshooting

"Apple can't verify
this app."

BhittePatro is not on the Mac App Store yet, so macOS blocks it by default. It is a one-time, two-minute fix.

This is a one-time process. Once you allow BhittePatro, it opens normally on every subsequent launch. macOS is cautious because the app is not signed with a paid Apple Developer certificate — the app itself is open source and safe to inspect.
  • 01
    Attempt to open BhittePatro
    Double-click BhittePatro.app in Finder. macOS shows: "Apple could not verify 'BhittePatro' is free of malware." Click Done. Do not move it to Trash.
  • 02
    Open System Settings
    Click the Apple logo in the top-left menu bar, then select System Settings...
  • 03
    Go to Privacy & Security
    In the left sidebar, click Privacy & Security.
    Apple Menu › System Settings › Privacy & Security
  • 04
    Find the blocked app notice
    Scroll to the Security heading at the bottom of the panel. Look for: "'BhittePatro' was blocked to protect your Mac." Click Open Anyway next to it.
  • 05
    Authenticate
    A confirmation dialog appears. Click Open Anyway again. macOS will ask for your admin password or Touch ID. Provide it to proceed.
  • 06
    BhittePatro opens — you are done
    The app launches normally. From this point on, double-clicking opens it with no warnings. These steps will not be needed again.
In macOS Tahoe 26, the right-click "Open" workaround no longer bypasses Gatekeeper. You must use the Privacy & Security settings path below.
  • 01
    Attempt to open BhittePatro
    Double-click BhittePatro.app in Finder. Tahoe shows: "Apple could not verify 'BhittePatro' is free of malware that may harm your Mac or compromise your privacy." Click Done. Do not move it to Trash.
  • 02
    Open System Settings
    Click the Apple logo in the top-left menu bar and choose System Settings...
  • 03
    Go to Privacy & Security
    In the left sidebar, click Privacy & Security.
    Apple Menu › System Settings › Privacy & Security
  • 04
    Find the blocked app notice
    Scroll down to the Security section at the bottom. You will see: "'BhittePatro' was blocked to protect your Mac." Click Open Anyway directly next to that message.
  • 05
    Confirm in the second dialog
    A new warning dialog appears with the text "Apple is not able to verify that it is free from malware." Click Open Anyway once more.
  • 06
    Enter your administrator password
    macOS will prompt: "You are attempting to open an app that may cause harm to your Mac." Enter your admin username and password and click OK. Touch ID is not available at this step on Tahoe.
  • 07
    BhittePatro opens — you are done
    The app launches. Future launches are a normal double-click with no warnings. These steps will not be needed again.
